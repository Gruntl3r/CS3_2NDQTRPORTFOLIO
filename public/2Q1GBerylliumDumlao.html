the winterfox - a white fox



In the game called minecraft there is a creature called a winterfox

and this fox is a variant of its cousin, the normal fox!

Like its cousin, it can jump, curl up and hunt for food all on his own

but what makes it unique is that it is exclusive to snow biomes!



When left alone it will curl up into a ball and sleep, so cute!

but if you make the mistake of coming to close they WILL run away so be careful!

Along with their skitishness, they also like to snack on berries or hold items

They are even smart enough to know what item has more value than others!
